Dashboard

When you sign in, you land on your home screen — a starting point
tailored to your role. It puts the work you do most within easy reach and
gives you an at-a-glance sense of what needs attention.

A view that fits your role

Different roles need different things, so the home screen adapts:

Role What’s front and centre
Operations Today’s appointments, patient registration, admissions and payments
Doctor Your appointment queue and admitted patients
Nurse / Staff Admitted patients and ward information
Pharmacy Stock status, prescription lookup and billing
Tenant Admin Setup, staff, billing and the activity log

At-a-glance numbers

From your lists you can quickly gauge the state of the day — for example:

  • How many appointments are scheduled, completed, cancelled or
    marked no-show
  • Which bills are still unpaid
  • Which beds are vacant or occupied

Every list supports paging and filters, so you can narrow down to exactly
what you need.

Global search

A single search box helps you find things fast, wherever you are.

Search sees what your role sees

Search results are limited to the records your role is allowed to view, and
always stay within your own organisation.

Depending on your role, search can find:

  • Patients — by name, MRN, phone or email
  • Doctors — by name, specialisation or registration number
  • Appointments — by patient name, MRN, reason or beneficiary
  • Bills — by description, patient name or MRN
  • Hospitals & departments — by name, branch or city
  • Medicines — by name, code, strength or form
Two characters is enough

Start typing at least two characters and MedOps shows the closest matches
across the categories you can access.
