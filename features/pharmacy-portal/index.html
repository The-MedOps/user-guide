Pharmacy Portal

The Pharmacy Portal is where the pharmacy team works. It brings together
stock, supplier deliveries, prescription dispensing and billing — all for
your own pharmacy.

Who uses this

The Pharmacy team. Everything you see is specific to the pharmacy you
sign in to.

Stock and prices

See what’s on hand at a glance — each medicine’s quantity and selling price —
and search by name, code or strength.

  • Set a selling price for any medicine. If it isn’t stocked yet, this
    creates the entry ready for its first delivery.
  • Fill in medicine details like form and strength when they’re missing,
    without leaving the portal.

Receiving supplies

When stock arrives from a supplier, record the delivery so your stock stays
accurate. A supply record captures:

  • Supplier name and an optional reference (such as the supplier’s
    invoice or delivery-note number)
  • An optional note
  • The medicines received — each with a quantity and selling price, and an
    optional purchase cost for your own records

Recording a supply adds the quantities to your stock and can update the
selling price for each medicine.

Dispensing against a prescription

  1. Enter the prescription number. MedOps shows the prescribed medicines
    next to your current stock and prices.
  2. Check the warning, if any. If this prescription has already been
    billed, MedOps flags it so you don’t bill it twice.
  3. Create the invoice. Prices come from your stock and tax is added
    automatically; your stock is reduced as you dispense.
  4. Take payment by cash or bank transfer.

Walk-in sales

You can also sell over the counter to customers who aren’t registered
patients — just record the customer’s name and the medicines, and bill the
same way.

What’s on an invoice

  • An invoice number assigned automatically
  • The customer — the patient (with MRN) or the walk-in customer’s name
  • The prescription reference, when it’s a prescription sale
  • The medicines, each with quantity, price and line total
  • The subtotal, the CGST and SGST amounts, and the total
  • The payment method (cash or bank transfer) and status

You can list and search your invoices, and open any invoice to view or print
it.

Built-in protection

Accurate stock and pricing, always
  • Stock never goes negative. If two sales reach for the last units, only
    one succeeds — the other is told stock has run out.
  • Prices are set by your pharmacy, not entered at the till, so billing
    stays consistent.
  • Tax is recorded per invoice, so later rate changes never alter past
    invoices.

Reference

While receiving supplies or billing, you can browse the shared
Medicines Catalogue for medicine details. For the full
flow, see Pharmacy Dispensing.
