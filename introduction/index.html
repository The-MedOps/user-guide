Overview

MedOps HMS is a complete hospital management platform. It connects the
people and tasks involved in running a hospital — registering patients,
booking appointments, seeing doctors, prescribing and dispensing
medicines, admitting and discharging inpatients, and collecting payments —
around a single, shared patient record.

One record, one workflow

Most hospital software keeps each department on its own island. MedOps is
different: a patient is registered once, and everything that happens
afterwards is linked back to that one record.

  • The front desk registers a patient and books an appointment.
  • The doctor opens that appointment, records the consultation, and writes
    a prescription.
  • The system creates the consultation bill automatically.
  • The pharmacy pulls up the same prescription to dispense and bill the
    medicines.
  • If the patient is admitted, the ward records rounds and the stay is
    tracked to discharge.

Everyone sees a consistent picture, and nothing has to be re-typed from
one screen to the next.

How MedOps is organised

Organisation, hospitals, and people

MedOps is built for healthcare organisations. Your organisation can
run one hospital or several. All of your hospitals, staff, patients and
records live inside your organisation and are kept completely separate
from every other organisation on the platform.

  • Your organisation is the top level — created when your administrator
    first signs up.
  • Hospitals belong to your organisation. Each hospital has its own
    departments, doctors, beds, patients and pharmacy.
  • People (your staff) are given a role that decides what they can see
    and do, and are usually tied to a specific hospital.

Who uses MedOps

MedOps supports six roles, each tailored to a job in the hospital:

Role Who they are
Tenant Admin Owns the organisation; sets up hospitals, staff and preferences
Doctor Sees patients, records consultations, writes prescriptions, does ward rounds
Nurse Supports bedside and ward care
Staff General hospital staff
Operations Front desk — registration, appointments, admissions, payments
Pharmacy Runs the pharmacy: stock, dispensing and billing

See Roles at a Glance for what each role
can do, or jump straight to your Role Guide.

What you can do

Run the front desk

Register patients, book and reschedule appointments, admit and
discharge inpatients, and take payments.

Deliver care

Record consultations and vitals, prescribe medicines, set follow-ups,
and round on admitted patients.

Run the pharmacy

Receive stock, set prices, dispense against prescriptions, and bill
walk-in customers — all with tax handled for you.

Stay in control

Manage hospitals, departments, beds and staff, and review a complete
activity log of who did what and when.
