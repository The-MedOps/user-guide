Key Features

Here is the full picture of what MedOps HMS offers. Every feature shares
the same patient record and the same set of roles, so the pieces fit
together end to end.

Front desk & patients

Patient registration

Register a patient once with their personal and contact details. Each
patient gets a unique Medical Record Number (MRN) automatically.
Learn more →

Appointments

Book appointments against a doctor’s real availability. MedOps blocks
double-bookings and bookings during a doctor’s leave.
Learn more →

Clinical care

Consultations

Doctors record notes and follow-up dates. Completing a consultation
creates the prescription and the consultation bill in a single step.
Learn more →

Prescriptions

Clear, printable prescriptions with medicines, dosage, frequency and
duration — ready for the patient and the pharmacy.
Learn more →

Admitted patients (IPD)

Admit patients to specific beds, record ward rounds and vitals, add
inpatient medications, transfer beds, and discharge.
Learn more →

My appointments & availability

Doctors see their own appointment queue and set their weekly working
hours, leave and time-off.
Learn more →

Pharmacy

Medicines catalogue

A shared, searchable list of medicines used across prescriptions and
pharmacy stock.
Learn more →

Pharmacy portal

Receive stock, set selling prices, dispense against prescriptions,
handle walk-in sales, and bill with tax calculated for you.
Learn more →

Money & oversight

Billing & payments

Consultation and pharmacy bills are generated for you. Record
payments, track what’s unpaid, and cancel mistakes.
Learn more →

Activity log

A complete, searchable record of who did what and when across the
organisation.
Learn more →

Setup & everyday tools

Hospitals & departments

Set up each hospital and its departments, then build out floors, rooms
and beds.
Learn more →

Staff management

Add and manage doctors and operations (front-desk) staff, and invite
them by email.
Learn more →

Dashboard

A role-aware home screen that puts your day’s work and key numbers
within easy reach.
Learn more →

Global search

Find patients, doctors, appointments, bills and more from one search
box — limited to what your role can see.
Learn more →

Built-in safeguards

Designed to prevent everyday mistakes
  • No double-booking — you can’t book a doctor who is busy, on leave, or off-duty.
  • No lost charges — consultation and pharmacy bills are created automatically.
  • No stock surprises — dispensing reduces stock and is blocked when stock runs out.
  • Nothing slips through — every important action is recorded in the activity log.
