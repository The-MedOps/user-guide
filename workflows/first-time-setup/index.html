First-Time Setup

This guide walks a Tenant Admin through setting up MedOps from scratch.
Work through it in order — each step builds on the one before.

Who this is for

You’ll need the Tenant Admin role. Your organisation was created when you
first signed up; now you’ll fill it with hospitals, wards, people and
preferences.

  1. Create your first hospital
    Add the hospital’s name, location, type, the departments it offers, and
    its compliance details (licences and tax identification).
    Hospitals & Departments →
  2. Add departments
    Create the clinical departments inside the hospital (Cardiology, ICU, OPD,
    and so on).
  3. Build the ward layout
    Add floors, then rooms (with their room type), then beds. This is the
    capacity you’ll admit inpatients into.
  4. Set room pricing
    Give each room type a daily tariff (base rent, nursing, doctor visit and
    amenity charges) so inpatient charges are consistent.
  5. Invite your doctors
    Add each doctor with their credentials, department and consultation fee.
    They’ll get an email to set their password.
    Managing Doctors →
  6. Invite your front desk
    Add your operations (front-desk) staff and assign them to a hospital.
    Operations Team →
  7. Set up the pharmacy
    Create one or more pharmacies with their tax rates, then add pharmacy
    logins for the team.
    Pharmacies →
  8. Stock the medicines catalogue
    Add the medicines your hospital uses (doctors can also add medicines while
    prescribing).
    Medicines Catalogue →
  9. Set your preferences
    Choose your branding and logo, your document code prefix, your inpatient
    billing rules, and the inactivity sign-out time.
    Tenant Admin guide →
You’re ready to go live

Once doctors have set their availability and the front desk is trained,
you’re ready to register patients and book the first appointments.
