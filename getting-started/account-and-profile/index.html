Your Account & Profile

Your profile holds your personal details and sign-in settings. You can view
it any time and update the parts that are yours to manage.

What’s on your profile

Your profile shows:

  • Name
  • Email address — this is also how you sign in
  • Phone number
  • Location — country, state and city
  • Role — for example, Doctor or Operations
  • Email verification status — whether your email has been confirmed
  • Account status — active, inactive, or suspended
  • Inactivity sign-out time — how long until you’re signed out when idle

Editing your details

You can update the following yourself:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Country, state and city
Some details are managed for you

Your email address, role, organisation, and hospital
assignment are set by your administrator and can’t be changed from your
profile. If any of these are wrong, ask your Tenant Admin to update them.

Changing your password

If you know your current password, you can change it at any time:

  1. Open the change-password screen from your profile.
  2. Enter your current password.
  3. Enter a new password. It must be at least 8 characters with an
    uppercase letter, a number, and a special character — and it must be
    different from your current one.
  4. Save. For your security, changing your password signs you out on
    every device, so sign in again with the new password.
Signed in with a one-time code?

If you usually sign in with an emailed code rather than a password, use
Forgot password on the sign-in page to set a password instead — the
change-password screen needs an existing password to work.

Keeping your account secure

  • Use a password you don’t use anywhere else.
  • Always Sign out on shared or public computers.
  • If you think someone else knows your password, change it right away —
    this immediately signs out every device.
