Operations (Front Desk)

As an Operations user you run the front desk. You’re the first point of
contact for patients: registering them, booking their appointments,
admitting and discharging inpatients, and taking payments.

Your toolkit

Register patients

Add new patients with their details. Each gets a unique Medical Record
Number automatically.
Open →

Book appointments

Schedule visits against a doctor’s real availability — MedOps prevents
double-booking and bookings during leave.
Open →

Admit & discharge

Admit patients to beds, transfer them between beds, and process
discharges.
Open →

Take payments

Record payments on consultation bills, track what’s unpaid, and cancel
bills created in error.
Open →

Booking an appointment

  1. Find or register the patient. Search by name, MRN, phone or email;
    if they’re new, register them first.
  2. Pick the doctor and time. MedOps shows the doctor’s available slots
    and hides times that are already booked or blocked.
  3. Add the reason for the visit (and any beneficiary details for an
    outpatient visit booked on someone’s behalf).
  4. Confirm. The appointment appears in the doctor’s queue.
MedOps catches conflicts for you

You can’t book a doctor in the past, outside their working hours, during
their leave, or on a slot that’s already taken — so clashes are avoided
before they happen.

Admitting a patient

  1. Choose the patient and an available bed.
  2. Select the admitting doctor and record the reason for admission.
  3. Capture payment details — any advance deposit, the payment type
    (self-pay, cashless or corporate), and insurance information if relevant.
  4. Confirm. The bed is marked occupied and the patient is ready for
    ward rounds and inpatient medicines.

For the full walkthrough, see
An Inpatient Admission.

Everyday helpers

  • Global search finds patients, appointments and bills in one place.
  • Reschedule or cancel appointments when plans change.
  • Mark no-shows so doctors’ queues stay accurate.
