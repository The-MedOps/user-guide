Pharmacy

As a Pharmacy user you work in the Pharmacy Portal — your dedicated
space for stock, dispensing and billing. You always work within your own
pharmacy, so everything you see is specific to where you sign in.

Dispensing against a prescription

  1. Enter the prescription number (the patient brings it, or it’s shared
    from the consultation).
  2. Review the medicines. MedOps shows the prescribed items alongside
    your current stock and prices, and warns you if this prescription has
    already been billed.
  3. Create the invoice. Prices come from your stock and tax is added
    automatically. Your stock goes down as you dispense.
  4. Take payment by cash or bank transfer and hand over the medicines.

For the full walkthrough, see
Pharmacy Dispensing.

Keeping stock accurate

  • Receiving supplies increases your stock and can update the selling
    price for each medicine.
  • Selling and dispensing decrease your stock automatically.
  • MedOps won’t let you sell more than you have — if two sales race for
    the last units, only one goes through.
Tax is handled for you

Your pharmacy’s tax rates are applied to each invoice automatically, and
each invoice keeps a copy of the rates used at the time — so later changes
never rewrite past invoices.

Good to know

  • You can browse the shared medicines catalogue for reference while
    receiving stock or billing.
  • Pharmacy records (stock, supplies and invoices) are private to your
    pharmacy.
